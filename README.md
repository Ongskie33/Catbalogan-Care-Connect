# Catbalogan-Care-Connect1
Catbalogan Care Connect is a mobile application designed to revolutionize healthcare management in Catbalogan City. This innovative hospital management system simplifies patient access to essential services, making healthcare more efficient and convenient. The app allows users to reserve hospital rooms seamlessly, view detailed information about doctors, including their specialties and availability, and track their medical conditions with ease. Patients can also manage their admission schedules and stay informed about their treatment plans. Additionally, the app provides a transparent overview of billing details, ensuring a hassle-free experience. Catbalogan Care Connect is a vital tool for enhancing communication between patients and healthcare providers, ultimately fostering better healthcare outcomes for the community.
